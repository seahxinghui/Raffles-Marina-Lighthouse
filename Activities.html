Activities

Other than taking enjoying the beautiful views and sunset, there are many other fun and exciting activties to take part in! Fishing-enthusiasts would be happy to know they can enjoy fishing along the breakwater from 7am to 12am and Raffles Marina hotel guests are entitled to complimentary fishing bait, free storage and cooking of catch at Captain's Table Chinese Restaurant.

Private cruise chatering services are also available with BoatsBoatsBoats, providing you with a luxurious and relaxing ride at sea. Raffles Marina do organise short, medium and long range cruises. ABA Anambas Cruise visits some of Indonesia's most beautiful islands such as Pualu Bawah.

Boating courses are also available. Additionally, Raffles Marina offers sailing faciltiies and organises events such as competitive racing and fun races.
