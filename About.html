About

Situated in the west is Singapore's secret lighthouse - Raffles Marina Lighthouse. Standing at 12-metres, this lighthouse built in 1994 is the only lighthouse in Singapore which is owned and maintained privately. The lighthouse is located at the tip of Raffles Marina Club breakwater stands, overlooking Tuas Second Link.

The lighthouse is still in operation and flashes every 10 seconds. Although the lighthouse is situated in Raffles Marina Club, it is open to public without any charges! It is free for all to visit. At the Raffles Marina Lighthouse, not only can you catch the beautiful view of the sunset at dusk but also take beautiful photos with the lighthouse along with the skyline of Johor Bahru in the background.

It is a popular photo-taking spot! Besides taking photographs, Raffles Marina has lots to offer! There are dining options ranging from fine dining to casual al-fresco dining. You can also visit the Raffles Marina hotel for a relaxing vacation. There are also seasports and activities offered by Raffles Marina Club.
