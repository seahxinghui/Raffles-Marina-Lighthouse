Facilities

There are many club facilities available. These include an Aerobics Room, Children's Playroom, Gymnasium, Massage Chairs, Member's Lounge (Members only), Swimming Pool & Jacuzzi (Members only). For those who wish to have a longer stay at Raffles Marina may visit the Raffles Marina Hotel where they offer standard and deluxe rooms. There are also many other venues and facilities available such as the Pool Deck, Lawn, Bistro Terrace, Theatrette, Compass Ballroom, Chartroom, Briderooms, Boardroom and Atrium.
